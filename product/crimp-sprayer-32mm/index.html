  • Specifications
  • Safe Material – Our 32mm crimp sprayer is made of PP (polypropylene) plastic, BPA free and no lead, it can be used safely in various situations.
  • Standard Size – It comes with 32mm thread size. You can use it on any bottles with a cap size of 32mm.
  • Benefits of Using – Crimp sprayer features a bayonet fitting design instead of screw thread. It is ideal for dispensing body care lotions. The transparent PP over cap on top can effectively prevent dust and avoid accidental touch, so as to keep the internal products clean.
  • Colors Available – Our stock color is usually white or transparent. Any custom color such as translucent or solid color can be manufactured immediately if you’re in need of bulk purchase.
  • Dip Tube Length – Dip tube of crimp sprayer will be cut as per your bottle height. The proper tube length makes sure the product inside your bottle will be not wasted.
  • Quality Assurance – We focus on product quality. We perform strict quality check & testing before delivering quality sprayers to our customers, including appearance check, wall thickness check, thread matching test, liquid leakage test, and stability of liquid output per stroke. We can provide you free samples for quality checking before you officially place an order.
  • Certificated – The crimp sprayer meets FDA & CE standards.
  • Factory Direct – We are the manufacturer located in Taizhou City, Zhejiang Province, China. All products are wholesale directly from our factory.
  • Delivery Time – We always keep a certain number of the crimp sprayer in stock. For MOQ orders, immediate shipping is available. For order purchased in large quantities, with our strong production capacity and advanced production technology, it can also be delivered to customers quickly.
  • Outer Packing Carton – By default, we use our standard carton to pack and ship the sprayers. The principle of our carton outer packaging is to save space as much as possible, so that to help our customers save shipping fee. But in some situations, you may require custom sizes and printing content for the outer carton packing, for example, you will supply the products directly to your retails or end customers. We can provide this service, but please note, it is only for large quantity purchase orders.
  • Shipping Assistance – Since our products are sold globally, you may not be familiar with the import process, don’t worry, we can provide assistance in the entire process from product delivery to your receipt of the goods, including assistance with shipping, customs declaration and customs clearance.
